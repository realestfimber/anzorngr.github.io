about us

Anl(Anzor Nigeria Limited) is an indigenous company incorporated in the year 1995. The company is positioned to play vital role in the global oil and gas industry. Anzor (Nigeria) Limited is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified. The ultimate focus of the company is to provide technical and logistics support services for the oil & gas and energy sectors. The company envisions enviable stake in the global oil & gas sector. Anzor (Nigeria) Limited in order to sustain her foregoing performance in the industry has established strong management team to drive the company vision, mission, policies and strategies for continual high performance.
