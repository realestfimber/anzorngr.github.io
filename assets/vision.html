OUR VISION

Anzor (Nigeria) Limited vision is to hold a formidable position as a technical and logistics support service firm in the oil and gas industry. Also to contribute meaningfully to the growth of the industry and to regarded as a dependable partner by our clients, stakeholders, business associates and our host communities.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to offer the best engineering/technical and logistics support services to our clients at the best possible price without compromising standards and professionalism.

OUR VALUES

Anzor (Nigeria) Limited renders quality technical and logistics support services that meet the highly technologically driven services required by the oil and gas industry. In order to achieve these the company maintains:

  1. Good working relationship with our clients, supplier and host communities to help improve the standard of living and quality of life.
  2. Deployment of available current technology in execution of projects for clients in a cost effective manner.
  3. The policy that all personnel involved in project execution work under good and safe environment.

Our values are furthered in the one word “CORE”

  • C- COMMITMENT
  • O-OBLIGATION
  • R- RESPECT FOR QUALITY
  • E- EFFECTIVENESS
